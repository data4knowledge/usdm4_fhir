    guidance: Trial interventions are all pre-specified, investigational and non-investigational medicinal products, medical devices or other interventions intended for the participants during the trial. The investigational trial intervention is the product used in the trial as part of trial objectives. Description of investigational trial intervention is provided in Section 6.1. Other trial interventions that are not part of trial objectives (not an investigational role in this trial) are described in Section 6.9 Description of Non-investigational trial interventions. Any regional requirements should be noted in the appropriate subsections. Provide an overview of investigational and non-investigational trial interventions.
