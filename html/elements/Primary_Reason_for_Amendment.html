    • text: The rationale for the change(s) to, or formal clarification of, a protocol.

Changes to primary estimand, endpoints, or related measures should be listed as a change of strategy. If none of the choices apply, choose “other” and provide a description. If no secondary reason, indicate “not applicable” for the secondary reason.
